Batter data input will be formatted as follows

Line 1: FirstName LastName Bats
Line 2: Numbers vs. Lefthanded pitching
Line 3: Numbers vs. Righthanded pitching
Line 4: Defense.  First value is number of positions player can play, followed by position data
Line 5: Speed/Misc data for bunt and injury rating

Pitcher data input will be formatted as follows

Line 1: FirstName LastName ThrowingArm StaminaInformation
Line 2: Defense and Symbols
Line 3: Numbers vs. Lefthanded batters
Line 4: Numbers vs. Righthanded batters
